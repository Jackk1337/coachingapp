## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Account Information
When you create an Account, we collect:
- Email address;
- Name (first name and last name);
- Display name;
- Profile photo (if provided through Google Sign-In);
- Authentication credentials (managed by Firebase Authentication);

### 3.2 Profile Information
During onboarding and through your use of the Service, we collect:
- Date of birth;
- Gender;
- Country and timezone;
- Height (in centimetres);
- Weight (in kilograms);
- Body fat percentage;
- Activity level (e.g., Sedentary, Lightly Active, Moderately Active, Very Active, Extremely Active);
- Experience level (e.g., Novice, Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced);
- Handle or username (for community features);

### 3.3 Health and Fitness Data
We collect comprehensive health and fitness information, including:

**Workout Data:**
- Exercise logs (exercises performed, sets, repetitions, weight lifted);
- Rate of Perceived Exertion (RPE);
- Workout dates and durations;
- Workout programmes and routines.

**Food Diary Data:**
- Meals logged (food items, quantities, times);
- Calorie intake;
- Macronutrient information (protein, carbohydrates, fats);
- Barcode scanning data (for food identification);
- Meal photos (if uploaded).

**Cardio Data:**
- Cardio session logs (type, duration, distance);
- Heart rate information;
- Calories burned during cardio sessions.

**Daily Check-in Data:**
- Daily weight measurements;
- Step counts;
- Sleep duration and quality;
- Training status (whether you trained or did cardio);
- Calorie goal achievement status;
- Other health metrics you choose to log.

**Weekly Check-in Data:**
- Average weight, steps, and sleep for the week;
- Workout and cardio goal achievement;
- Appetite, energy levels, and digestion;
- Workout reflections and achievements;
- Challenges and social events;
- Confidence levels and future plans.

**Progress Data:**
- Historical weight, body measurements, and fitness metrics;
- Progress charts and visualisations;
- Weekly summaries and trends.

**Water and Supplements Data:**
- Daily water intake;
- Supplement logs (types, dosages, schedules).

### 3.4 Goals and Preferences
We collect information about your fitness goals and preferences:
- Goal type (e.g., Lose Weight, Gain Strength, Gain Weight);
- Calorie targets and limits;
- Macronutrient goals (protein, carbohydrates, fats);

### 3.7 AI Coaching Data

### 3.8 Community Data

## 10. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 10.1 Right of Access
You have the right to:
- Request copies of your personal data;
- Receive information about how we process your data;
- Understand what personal data we hold about you.

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com with the subject line "Data Access Request". We will respond within one month (or up to two months for complex requests).

### 10.2 Right to Rectification
You have the right to:
- Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data;
- Update your personal information through your Account settings or by contacting us.

**How to Exercise:** You can update most information through your Account settings. For other corrections, contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com.

### 10.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances, including when:
- The data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected;
- You withdraw consent and there is no other legal basis for processing;
- You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds;
- The data has been unlawfully processed.

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com or delete your Account through the Service settings. Note that we may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

### 10.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, such as when:
- You contest the accuracy of the data;
- The processing is unlawful but you do not want the data deleted;
- We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims.

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com with your request.

### 10.5 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to:
- Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format;
- Transfer your data to another service provider;
- Request that we transmit your data directly to another service (where technically feasible).

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com with your request. We will provide your data in a standard format (e.g., JSON or CSV) within one month.

### 10.6 Right to Object
You have the right to object to processing of your personal data based on:
- Legitimate interests (we will stop processing unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds);
- Direct marketing (we will stop processing immediately);
- Processing for scientific or historical research or statistical purposes (in certain circumstances).

**How to Exercise:** Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com or use the opt-out mechanisms we provide (e.g., unsubscribe links in marketing emails).

### 10.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making
You have the right to:
- Not be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing (including profiling) that produce legal or similarly significant effects;
- Request human intervention in automated decision-making processes;
- Express your point of view and contest automated decisions.

**Note:** Our AI coaching messages are generated using automated processing, but they do not produce legal or similarly significant effects. They are recommendations that you can choose to follow or ignore. You always have the right to request that we review or modify AI-generated content.

### 10.8 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise any of your rights:
1. Contact us at hello@rallyfitapp.com;
2. Clearly specify which right you wish to exercise;
3. Provide sufficient information to verify your identity (we may request additional verification);
4. We will respond within one month (or up to two months for complex requests).
